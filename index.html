01 · OVERVIEW

Introduction

GloomDrop is a mobile game developed by Leonardo Gabriel da Silva (publicly known as Leonardo Mondaine). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you play GloomDrop on Android devices.

By downloading and playing GloomDrop, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

02 · DATA WE COLLECT

Information Collection

GloomDrop is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary to operate the game and serve advertisements.

  • Advertising data — GloomDrop uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and interaction data to serve relevant ads.
  • Game progress — Your level progress and settings are stored locally on your device only. We do not store this data on external servers.
  • Crash reports — Anonymous crash data may be collected automatically by the Android platform to help us fix bugs.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information directly.

03 · ADVERTISING

Google AdMob

GloomDrop displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob is operated by Google LLC and is subject to Google's own privacy policy.

  • AdMob may use cookies and device identifiers to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • AdMob may collect data such as device type, OS version, and general location for ad targeting purposes.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings under Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

For more information, visit: policies.google.com/privacy

04 · CHILDREN

Children's Privacy

GloomDrop is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove such information.
